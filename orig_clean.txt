Explore our therapeutic philosophy or biographical path below. Our Philosophy Biographical Path A personal experience. A path to awareness. Besides relieving people of the problem that originally brought them into my clinic, I seek to give them something that will make them need my services less in the future. A good treatment, for me, is one that makes the client stronger, and strength is achieved through experience and awareness. With that goal in mind, it is important for me to give the client experience, not just a service. There are many ways to get healthier, stronger and happier. Taking care of oneself takes part in all avenues of life. From the food we eat to our daily habits and to the way we treat others. The experiences we go through in the clinic affect all of these layers, and when our experience is positive, these positive changes cascade to other aspects of our lives. So with that notion in mind, I try to pass the knowledge that was entrusted in me to my patients, and wherever this knowledge is insufficient, I will do my best to refer the client to a practitioner who's system is better equipped for the job. All Articles Movement Bodywork Nutrition Good to Know Frequently Asked Questions Practical details and guidelines before you arrive for your touch therapy or movement lesson, translated from our clinic files. What should I wear to my treatment? Clients are kindly requested to arrive in comfortable, loose clothing. Please bring a clean pair of socks for touch treatments (like Shiatsu), and suitable active shoes for light movement exercises in case we practice Qigong in the garden. What happens during the first session? Your first treatment session frequently includes a comprehensive clinical intake interview. This diagnostic process is necessary for me to understand the physical and emotional challenges you face, evaluate structural nodes, and customize the manual bodywork or movement therapy to your specific needs. Is there any post-treatment recovery advice? Touch and movement treatments continue to resonate and process in your nervous system long after the session finishes. It is highly recommended to set aside the hours immediately after your treatment for quiet rest and calming activity. An abrupt transition into high-intensity physical tasks or severe stress can significantly damp the treatment's results. What precautions should I take regarding clothes or makeup? Because sessions may include touch therapies on the face, please arrive without heavy makeup that could smudge. Additionally, we utilize organic massage oils and traditional Moxa (smokeless heat herb therapy). While we take every precaution, please wear clothes that you do not mind getting slightly oiled or catching a herbal scent. Can I drive home immediately after a deep session? Certain treatments address deep visceral layers and can trigger a deep vegetative state, making activities that require intense concentration (like driving a car or heavy motor control) slightly challenging right after. In these cases, taking a brief walk, a gentle Qigong sequence, or rest in our waiting area before departing is strongly advised. Contact &amp; Location Our practice is located in Basel, Switzerland.
